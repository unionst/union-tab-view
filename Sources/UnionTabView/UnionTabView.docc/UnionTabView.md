# ``UnionTabView``

A SwiftUI package that brings iOS 26's Liquid Glass tab bar with fully custom tab item views to your apps.

## Overview

UnionTabView gives you the beautiful floating glass effect of iOS 26's native tab bar while allowing you to use any custom SwiftUI view for tab itemsâ€”including profile pictures, custom icons, animated badges, and more.

![UnionTabView Demo](demo.gif)

The standard iOS 26 `TabView` only supports system-provided tab items. UnionTabView solves this limitation by recreating the liquid glass aesthetic with full `@ViewBuilder` support, giving you complete creative control over your tab bar appearance.
